# Call Closing Snippets

Professional call-ending scripts for different scenarios.

## Successful Resolution

> "I'm glad we could get that sorted out for you today, [Name]. Is there anything else I can help you with before we wrap up?"

> "Perfect, so to summarize — [recap actions taken]. You should see [expected result] within [timeframe]. Anything else?"

## Appointment Set

> "Excellent! You're all set for [date] at [time] with [person/team]. You'll receive a confirmation [email/text] shortly. Is there anything you'd like to prepare before the meeting?"

## Transfer

> "I'm going to connect you with [Name] in [Department] who can help you with that directly. Thank you for calling [Company], [Name]!"

## No Resolution — Follow-Up Needed

> "I want to make sure we get this fully resolved for you. I'll [action item] and follow up with you by [date/time]. Is [phone/email] the best way to reach you?"

## After Hours

> "Our office hours are [hours]. I've noted your message and someone from our team will call you back first thing [tomorrow/next business day]. Have a great evening!"

## Caller Wants to End

> "Thank you for calling [Company], [Name]. Have a wonderful [day/evening]! Goodbye."
